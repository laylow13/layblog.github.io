Model predictive control for quadrotor drone

Nolinear

Quadrotor model (FLU)

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\dot{p} = v

\

&\dot{v} = \frac{1}{m} \left( T - mg\vec{e}_3 + F_d \right)

\

&\dot{R} = R\hat{\Omega}

\

&\dot{\Omega} = J^{-1} \left( \Omega \times J\Omega + M + M_d \right)
\end{aligned}
$$

MPC algorithm

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\text{min}U &&\sum{k=0}^{N-1}(||x_k-x_{ref,k} ||{Q_x}^2 +||u_k-u{ref}||{R_u}^2)
+||x_N-x{ref,N}||{P}^2
\
&\text{s.t.} && x{k+1}=f_d(x_k,u_k),k=0,\dots,N-1
\
& && x_0=x(t_0)
\
& && u_{\text{min}}\leq u_k\leq u_{\text{max}}\ (u_k\in \mathbb{U}),k=0,\dots,N-1
\
& &&g(x_k)>0,k=1,\dots,N
\end{aligned}
$$
